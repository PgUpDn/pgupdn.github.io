Hi! I'm Xinyu Yang. This page is a small home on the internet for the projects I'm actively shaping across simulation, agent orchestration, and knowledge tools.

My public GitHub work currently centers on a clear thread: making complex technical systems easier to operate through better interfaces, better automation, and more usable workflows.

That ranges from a natural-language urban cooling platform, to a personal research wiki that compounds knowledge over time, to organizational multi-agent simulations inspired by real institutions.
